About Airport View Hotel

As an indigenous Ghanaian hospitality center of excellence, Airport View Hotel has almost two decades of experience in hospitality service delivery as well as contributing significantly to the development of tourism in Ghana.

The hotel was established on the 1st of April, 2002 and has through a series of expansions grown from a 12 room facility to a fully-fledged 3 Star hotel. Currently having a capacity of 80 comfortable rooms consisting of executive plus, executive, superior twin, standard twin, standard plus and standard rooms.

Airport View Hotel is very committed to consistent renovations reflecting current and modern trends and maintaining global service standards in the hospitality industry.
