About Western Region

The Western Region of Ghana is rich in natural resources, featuring beautiful rainforests, gold mines, and stunning coastlines. This region offers a unique blend of beach resorts, eco-tourism lodges, and mining town accommodations.

Key Locations

Sekondi-Takoradi • Tarkwa • Axim • Prestea • Half Assini
