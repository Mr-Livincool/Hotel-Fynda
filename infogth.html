About Golden Tulip Hotel

The refurbished Golden Tulip Accra is a first class African style business hotel with 238 rooms including perimeter suites, premier rooms, chalets and standard rooms.

Golden Tulip Hotels are superior first class hotels with international standards of comfort, service and facilities. They are also individual properties, which reflect the flavour of their location.

Situated in key locations, some are close to airports and local access routes, whilst others are located in business districts or close to local conference centres.
