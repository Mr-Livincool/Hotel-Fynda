About Eastern Region

The Eastern Region of Ghana is characterized by its beautiful mountains, waterfalls, and lush vegetation. Known as the "Garden City of West Africa," this region offers tranquil retreats and nature-focused accommodations.

Key Locations

Koforidua • Akosombo • Aburi • Nkawkaw • Suhum
