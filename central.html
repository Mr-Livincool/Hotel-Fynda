About Central Region

The Central Region of Ghana is known for its beautiful coastline, historic castles, and vibrant fishing communities. As the former center of the Gold Coast, this region offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty.

Key Locations

Cape Coast • Elmina • Winneba • Saltpond • Moree
