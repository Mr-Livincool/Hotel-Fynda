About Golden Tulip Hotel

The 4-star Golden Tulip Accra hotel is only 10 minutes' drive to the city center and government ministries. A free airport shuttle is available for the convenience of guests.

This African-styled hotel with a lovely ambience is delightfully landscaped over spacious grounds and offers Free WiFi in all hotel rooms and public areas.
